EMERG3D Props, prop maker

Premier destination for 3D-print creation

Emerg3D Props was born out of a unique passion and a deep-seated desire to join the prestigious 501st Legion. Faced with a lack of choices in Europe, I took the initiative to invest in a revolutionary technology: 3D printing. What began as a project to create just one costume quickly blossomed into an all-consuming passion for 3D printing.

Custom Cosplay on Demand

Today, Emerg3D Props specializes in creating custom-designed cosplay products, offering a diverse range of costumes, props, helmets and accessories tailored to individual orders. Our commitment to quality is evident in every commission, where design meets functionality to bring your dream cosplay to life. Each product, meticulously crafted, reflects our dedication to exceptional design and quality. Whether you're a cosplay enthusiast seeking a bespoke costume or looking for unique props, Emerg3D Props is your go-to destination, where your imagination is transformed into tangible, extraordinary creations.

3D Printed Homeware

At Emerg3D Props, our passion for 3D printing extends beyond cosplay, diving into the realm of home decor with our unique collection of 3D-printed homeware. Each item in our collection is a masterpiece of design, transforming everyday home essentials into personalized art pieces. Our dedication to innovative printing techniques allows us to offer a range of decor items, from intricate light fixtures to bespoke gifts, each infused with a personal touch. This collection not only exemplifies our commitment to quality and creativity but also offers a way to bring a piece of art into the comfort of your home. Whether you're looking for a special gift or a personalised accent to brighten your living space, our 3D-printed homeware collection offers a fusion of functionality and artistic design, elevating your home with a unique, printed charm.
